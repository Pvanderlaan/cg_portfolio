Window Dressing Visualizations

At Window Dressing I designed and modeled/textured/rendered various virtual retail shops. These designs were printed on real life scale up to 30 meters wide (rendering images of up to 30.000 pixels wide) and pasted on the front of vacant shops to improve the retail atmosphere and inspire other people what is possible with the empty shop space.

Client: Window Dressing
Year: 2014
Software: 3ds Max, VRay, Photoshop
Hardware: Kinect
